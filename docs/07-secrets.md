# 07 — Secrets & Lore Structure

The game has several optional secret chains. Casual players can finish the main story without them; deeper players uncover hidden truths.

## Secret Chain 1: The Bell Family

**Focus:** Ruth and Jonah Bell.

**How uncovered:**

- Preserve Ruth's letters.
- Deliver Jonah's replies.
- Find Bell House on the map.
- Decode knocking pattern.

**Reward:** Reveals Jonah did not die normally. He was one of the first erased children.

## Secret Chain 2: Eleanor's Classroom

**Focus:** Teacher and students.

**How uncovered:**

- Archive school letters.
- Find children's drawings.
- Decode classroom song.
- Match student names to missing persons.

**Reward:** Reveals children hid memories in mail to survive erasure.

## Secret Chain 3: The Previous Clerk

**Focus:** The player's predecessor.

**How uncovered:**

- Disobey policy.
- Open black-sealed letters.
- Find notes in filing room.
- Decode redacted employee file.

**Reward:** Unlocks secret ending path.

## Secret Chain 4: Government Erasure Program

**Focus:** Director Harlan Pike and official cover-up.

**How uncovered:**

- Read policy manuals.
- Compare official records to letters.
- Search supervisor office.
- Preserve evidence.

**Reward:** Reveals the Dead Letter Office was built to prevent Marrow Creek from being remembered.

## Secret Chain 5: Player Identity

**Focus:** Who the player is.

**How uncovered:**

- Hide personal letters.
- Restore map locations.
- Read letters addressed to "Current Clerk."
- Open employee file.

**Reward:** Reveals the player may be a former resident, survivor, or constructed identity.

## Content Target

The full game should contain roughly 20 optional secrets across these chains, layered as:

- Obvious story clues
- Optional character connections
- Hidden codes
- Environmental changes
- Repeated symbols
- Secret endings
- ARG-like details that are never required to enjoy the game
